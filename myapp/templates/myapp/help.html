Tips for Better Money Management.
Make the most out of Artha AI.
Record transactions regularly
Make it a habit to log your transactions daily or weekly. This ensures your financial data stays accurate and up-to-date.
Set realistic goals
When creating financial goals, make sure they're achievable based on your income and expenses. Start small and gradually increase your targets.
Use categories consistently
Always use the same categories for similar expenses. This makes your reports and charts more meaningful and easier to understand.
